# IconCraft How-To

## Open the app

Launch `IconCraft.app` on a Mac, or open the installer DMG and drag the app into Applications.

## Use the app

1. Upload one image.
2. Review the preview matrix.
3. Crop the icon if you want tighter framing.
4. Upscale the image if the source is smaller than 1024px.
5. Export the format you need.

Available exports:

- `.iconset`
- `.icns`
- `AppIcon.xcassets`
- `React Native iOS`

## Workflow checklist

The app includes a live checklist that updates as you work:

- Upload artwork
- Crop icon
- Upscale if needed
- Export bundle

## Share the app

Send the `.dmg` file to a Mac user. They can open it and drag `IconCraft.app` into Applications.
